# Commerce Policy

This policy governs ChornPlanet commerce, marketplace linking, product content, and premium/luxury product workflows.

## Draft-first commerce rule

Agents may draft:

- product descriptions
- product story posts
- marketplace copy
- TikTok scripts
- product-linking plans
- campaign hypotheses
- commerce page structures
- product schema proposals

Agents must not publish, modify, delist, price, or promote live products on external marketplaces without explicit approval.

## Marketplace priority

Commerce rollout priority:

1. TikTok
2. Shopee
3. Lazada
4. Amazon
5. Owned commerce / direct checkout when approved

## Product truth rule

Commerce content must not misrepresent:

- availability
- price
- discount
- shipping
- warranty
- brand ownership
- marketplace status
- product origin
- product material
- delivery timeline

If product data is unavailable, agents should create a content draft with placeholders and call out the missing fields.

## ChornPlanet commerce direction

Starting wedge:

- lady outfit
- gentleman outfit
- civilization clothing
- premium fashion storytelling
- luxury visual posts
- TikTok-first product links

Future expansion:

- premium lifestyle products
- grocery
- online supermarket
- Smart Food ordering
- personalized recommendations

## Approval required

Explicit approval is required for:

- external marketplace publishing
- product price changes
- inventory changes
- checkout/payment workflows
- customer/order mutations
- product claims that require verification
- affiliate link or revenue-tracking changes
